**NAME:**  
Golden Chickens  
  
**Description**:   
Golden Chickens is a Malware-as-a-Service (MaaS) provider uncovered by QuoScient Intelligence Operations Team (QuoINT). There are two new threat actors: GC01 and GC02. From August to October 2018, GC01 carried out 9 spear-phishing attacks against financial institutions. GC02 performed 5 spear-phishing attacks from November 2017 to July 2018, targeting India and America. In the latter case, there are some overlaps with Gobalt infrastructure in May 2018.
